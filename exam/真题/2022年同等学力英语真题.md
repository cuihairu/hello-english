### Part II Vocabulary（10 minutes, 10 points）

Directions: In this part there are ten sentences each with one word or phrase underlined. Choose the one from the four choices marked A, B, C and D that best keeps the meaning of the sentence. Mark your answer on the Answer Sheet.

**11.** Many people are living in temporary homes after days of intense rain that caused **substantial** damage in the country.
A. numerous
B. heavy
C. fearful
D. sufficient
**答案：B**

**12.** It is believed that **shielding** children from all kinds of obstacles in the name of love does little for their future success in life.
A. removing
B. shadowing
C. sheltering
D. covering
**答案：C**

**13.** To live every day to the full is a **recipe** for happiness.
A. secret
B. menu
C. purpose
D. pursuit
**答案：A**

**14.** One reason for learning a foreign language is that it provides a **lens** through which to view cultural differences.
A. camera
B. pattern
C. concept
D. perspective
**答案：D**

**15.** The city has been **virtually** paralyzed by sudden snowstorms.
A. nearly
B. quickly
C. undoubtedly
D. completely
**答案：D**

**16.** Being late for the seminar, she found it really difficult to **catch up with** the subjects which had already been discussed.
A. follow
B. discuss
C. support
D. determine
**答案：A**

**17.** The Olympic Games attempt to **transcend** national interests and bring together best international athletes for a shared future.
A. expand
B. exceed
C. regain
D. retain
**答案：B**

**18.** She went along the sunny hills at a leisurely pace as there was **ample** time for her idle expedition.
A. brief
B. extra
C. flexible
D. enough
**答案：D**

**19.** What measures need to be taken to **curb** greenhouse gas emissions?
A. monitor
B. evaluate
C. control
D. eliminate
**答案：C**

**20.** He has taken out a half-page advertisement in his local paper to **put across** his point.
A. repeat
B. prove
C. stress
D. explain
**答案：D**
