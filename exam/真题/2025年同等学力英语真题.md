### Part II Vocabulary（10 minutes, 10 points）

**Section A**

Directions: In this section there are 10 sentences, each with one word or phrase underlined. Choose one of the four choices that is closest in meaning to the underlined part.

11. The airline **suspend** flights to the island due to the volcanic ash clouds around the airport.

A. halted

B. managed

C. sustained

D. returned

> **参考答案：A**
>
> **解析：** 句中词 suspend 意为"暂停；中止；终止；悬"。A项 halt 意为"停止；暂停"；B项 manage 意为"管理，处理"；C项 sustain 意为"维持，保持"；D项 return 意为"返回；恢复；归还"。suspend 与 halt 意思最接近，故选A。
>
> **参考译文：** 由于机场周围有火山灰云，该航空公司暂停了飞往该岛的航班。

12. Scientists **collaborated** to create the secret of a rare plant species, leading to a groundbreaking discovery.

A. protect

B. keep

C. uncover

D. hide

> **参考答案：C**
>
> **解析：** 句中词 create 意为"创造；造成"。A项 protect 意为"保护；防护"；B项 keep 意为"保持；保守"；C项 uncover 意为"揭示；发现"；D项 hide 意为"隐藏；掩盖"。
>
> **参考译文：** 科学家们合作研究一种稀有植物的秘密，最终取得了一项突破性发现。

### Part V Text Completion（20 minutes, 20 points）

**题目示例：**

A. park themselves

B. along a street

C. on an icy road

Phrases:

56. A. park themselves

57. B. slipping

C. ...

Most cars already have some driverless technology like the Anti-lock Braking System (ABS). When a car is **(59) \_\_\_**, the ABS can tap the brakes quickly. The wheels turn slowly but do not stop. The driver can then steer the car to safety.

Many cars also have parallel parking technology. These cars can **(60) \_\_\_**. No driver is needed. As cars get more driverless technology, they will be safer. This may make driving safer than cars with a driver. Driverless cars can **(61) \_\_\_** on busy roads safer. Also, automated vehicles don't get sleepy or text on their phones while driving.

> **参考答案：** 56-61 BCA BAC

> **参考译文：** 大多数汽车已经拥有一些无人驾驶技术，如防抱死制动系统（ABS）。当汽车在结冰的路面上打滑时，ABS可以快速踩下刹车。车轮转动缓慢，但不会停止。然后司机可以把车开到安全的地方。许多汽车也有平行停车技术。这些车可以沿着街道自动停车。不需要司机。随着汽车获得更多的无人驾驶技术，它们将比有司机的汽车更安全。无人驾驶车在路上可以比有司机的汽车靠得更近。这可能会使在繁忙的道路上驾驶更安全。此外，自动驾驶汽车在驾驶时不会感到困倦或在手机上发短信。

## Paper Two（试卷二）（50 minutes）

### Part VI Translation（20 minutes, 10 points）

**英译汉原文：**

When you're enjoying the outdoors — whether you're at a local park or camping in the wilderness — it's important to minimize your impact on the environment around you. Anything you leave behind, even by accident, could be harmful to animals or plants. To reduce your impact on nature, practice the principles of "leave no trace." That means leaving an area exactly as you found it when you arrived. Always look around to make sure you picked up everything you brought with you. It's also best not to take items you find in nature. Rocks, shells, and sticks are part of the local ecosystem and should be left alone.

**参考译文：**

当你享受户外活动时，无论是在当地的公园还是在野外露营，尽量减少对周围环境的影响是很重要的。你留下的任何东西，即使是偶然的，也可能对动物或植物有害。为了减少你对自然的影响，请实践"不留痕迹"的原则。这意味着离开一个你刚到的地方时，要让它保持原样。总是环顾四周，确保你带的东西都拿好了。最好不要带走在大自然中找到的东西。岩石、贝壳和树枝是当地生态系统的一部分，不应该被打扰。

### Part VII Writing（30 minutes, 15 points）

**题目：** The Importance of Lifelong Learning（终身学习的重要性）

**参考范文：**

As is known to all, lifelong learning can play a crucial role in our success. Where there is lifelong learning, there are more hopes, vitality and development.

Concerning the above-mentioned philosophy, we have come up with various views on it as illustrated below. Initially, lifelong learning may endow us with more competence and chances to overcome difficulties and approach challenges, thus we can win others' favor and respect. Additionally, lifelong learning can help us carve out a way to success although the road lead to success is bumpy and full of inevitable setbacks. Finally, with the good fortune derived from lifelong learning, we can proceed more smoothly and realize our dreams more rapidly.

With its seemingly magic power, lifelong learning can not only better accelerate individuals' development, but serve as an efficient instrument for individuals to change the world. Lifelong learning can lead us to the destination we are eager to reach, bring out the best of us and keep us confident.
