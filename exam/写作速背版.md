# 写作速背版

> 适用场景：考前 10 分钟快速浏览，优先保证能写出来、结构稳、句子能直接套。

## 一、四段式万能结构

### 第一段：引题 + 观点

```text
There is no denying that [话题] has become a topic of growing concern in modern society. As far as I am concerned, [你的观点].
```

### 第二段：理由一

```text
First of all, [理由一]. For example, [例子]. This shows that [小结].
```

### 第三段：理由二

```text
In addition, [理由二]. More importantly, [进一步说明]. Therefore, [结果].
```

### 第四段：总结

```text
In conclusion, [重申观点]. Only in this way can we [积极结果].
```

---

## 二、必背开头句

1. There is no denying that this issue has become increasingly important.
2. Nowadays, this topic has aroused widespread concern.
3. With the rapid development of society, this issue has become a hot topic.
4. Different people hold different views on this issue.
5. As far as I am concerned, this issue is of great importance.

## 三、必背正文句

1. First and foremost, this can bring many benefits to individuals and society.
2. In addition, it helps improve efficiency and quality.
3. What's more, it enables people to make better choices.
4. One of the main reasons is that it can meet practical needs.
5. Another important reason is that it contributes to long-term development.
6. For instance, many people have already benefited from such changes.
7. This clearly shows that the issue should not be ignored.
8. Small actions may seem unimportant, but together they can make a big difference.

## 四、必背结尾句

1. In conclusion, this issue deserves our serious attention.
2. To sum up, we should adopt a rational attitude toward it.
3. It is necessary for us to take practical action.
4. Only in this way can we achieve sustainable development.
5. I am firmly convinced that a better future can be created through joint efforts.

## 五、最常用替换词

| 普通写法 | 更稳妥写法 |
|------|------|
| important | crucial / essential |
| good | beneficial / favorable |
| bad | harmful / negative |
| think | believe / hold the view that |
| cause | lead to / result in |
| solve | address / tackle |
| improve | enhance / promote |
| many | a large number of / a growing number of |
| more and more | increasingly |
| show | demonstrate / indicate |

## 六、常见题目起手句

| 题目类型 | 起手句 |
|------|------|
| The Importance of ... | There is no denying that ... is of great importance in modern society. |
| How to ... | It is necessary for us to take effective measures to ... |
| Can ... replace ...? | With the rapid development of ..., people begin to discuss whether ... can replace ... |
| Is ... the most important? | Different people hold different views on whether ... is the most important thing in life. |
| The Ideal ... | In my opinion, an ideal ... should meet the following requirements. |

## 七、五类主题万能句

### 1. 教育

- Lifelong learning has become increasingly important in the modern world.
- Education not only broadens our minds but also improves our ability to solve problems.

### 2. 科技

- Technology brings convenience and efficiency, but it also creates new challenges.
- We should make proper use of technology while avoiding its potential risks.

### 3. 环保

- Environmental protection is the responsibility of every individual.
- Only by taking practical action can we build a cleaner world.

### 4. 人生观

- What matters most is not whether we always win, but whether we continue to grow.
- True happiness comes from inner satisfaction rather than external competition.

### 5. 健康生活

- A healthy lifestyle is of great importance to both body and mind.
- Only by forming healthy habits can we enjoy a better quality of life.

## 八、考前最后检查

- 是否写了 4 段
- 是否有明确观点
- 是否至少 2 个理由
- 是否用了连接词
- 是否有总结句
- 是否接近 150 词
- 是否有明显语法错误
